By learning jyutping, you can know the exact pronunciation of a word, thus mispronunciation can be avoided.

Similar to Pinyin in Mandarin, you can easily know the pronunciation of a word in Cantonese by looking up its jyutping.

{{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-question" title="Why should I learn Jyutping?" %}}

Since Chinese is not a phonographic language, a Chinese character itself doesn't tell you its pronunciation. A system is required to organize and represent the pronunciations of the words, and that's exactly why Jyutping exists. It's an easy system which you can learn with not much effort.

{{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-keyboard" title="Jyutping helps you type" %}}

Knowing Jyutping, you can type Cantonese smoothly with Jyutping keyboards, the most natural and easiest way to type Cantonese.
